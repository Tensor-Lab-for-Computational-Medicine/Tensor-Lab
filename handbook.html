Chapter Trios (UMD & UCSF)

Strategy (Exec Director)

The "CEO". Owns institutional relationships and funding. Ensures data access is secured before summer starts.

Operations (Ops Director)

The "COO". Owns the application database, interview scheduling, and weekly standups. Keeps the trains running.

Technical (Tech Director)

The "CTO". Audits code quality, manages compute resources, and serves as the technical safety net for fellows.

Crisis Management

Things will go wrong. Here is how you handle it.

Scenario: The "Ghost" Fellow

Symptom: ML Fellow misses 2 standups in a row.
Action: 1. Direct DM check-in ("Is everything okay?").
2. If no response in 24h, email personal address.
3. If no response in 48h, cut them. Replace with waitlist candidate immediately. Do not drag it out.

Scenario: The "Data Wall"

Symptom: IRB is stuck in review at Week 2.
Action: Trigger the Backup Rule. Team starts working on public data proxy immediately while waiting. Do not let them sit idle.
